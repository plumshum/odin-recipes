Description

Fried peanuts are a staple in Asian households and part of the rich Chinese culture among noble households. This recipe can be quickly made in 10 minutes with barely any work needed.

Ingredients

  • Some sort of raw bag of peanuts
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Air Fryer
  • Air Fry safe paper

Steps

  1. Put paper in fryer
  2. Add peanus until bottom of paper cannot be seen. Try not to put too much as though they are multiple layers on peanuts
  3. Add oil. Flip the container a few times to ensure oil is spread everywhere
  4. Fry the peanuts for 7-9 minutes on 390 degrees farenheit
  5. Once time is up, put peanuts in a container of your choice and sprinkle salt.
  6. Wait for at lesaet 15 minutes for peanuts to cool down and fry 100%
