Description

Tomatoes and eggs are Hannah's favorite. It is a simple stir-fry dish that is common in many Chinese households.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Chives
  • Salt
  • Optional: Ketchup (However, Hannah HATES it)

Steps

  1. Cut the tomates into crescent-shaped pieces
  2. Crack a few egg yolks and mix in a bowl
  3. Create scrambled eggs first. Add salt
  4. Add the tomatoes in.
  5. As you stir the tomatoes, add the necessary soy sauce, oyster sauce, chives, and extra salt. How much is up to you
  6. Put a lid over the stove and wait until tomatoes have turn fairly squishy and soft
