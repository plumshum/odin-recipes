Description

Ants on a log is a common American snack. There is no cooking needed, and kids as young as three years old can make this snack super easily!

Ingredients

  • Celery sticks
  • Nut butter of your choice (i.e: peanut butter, sun butter, etc.)
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Cut celery sticks around the size of your finger
  2. Apply the butter on the stick, filling up the crevices
  3. Add as many raisins on top of the butter as you want.
